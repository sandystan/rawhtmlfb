How To Make Payment With TrueMoney

STEP 1️⃣ After selecting the game and top-up amount, select the TrueMoney payment method.Then enter your email address and click the "Buy Now" button

STEP 2️⃣ Make sure your order details are correct and click the "Confirm" button to continue

STEP 3️⃣ You will get a payment code. Inform the payment code when you want to make a payment at the mini market marked TrueMoney.You can also see payment method information at the bottom of the screen.If you want the code and payment instructions to be sent via email or SMS, please enter your email address or mobile number and click the "Submit" button

STEP 4️⃣ After the system has successfully sent an SMS or an email, you will see a notification. Then please click the "Closed" button

STEP 5️⃣ If you previously chose to send payment instructions via SMS, this is an SMS containing the payment number that you will receive.

STEP 6️⃣ Show the payment code to the cashier at the mini market marked TrueMoney and make a payment.
